# 1. Project Overview

AI Receptionist is an enterprise-grade intelligent virtual assistant designed to automate customer interactions across voice calls, websites, WhatsApp, email, and messaging platforms. The platform helps businesses deliver 24/7 customer support, appointment booking, lead qualification, and business automation.
